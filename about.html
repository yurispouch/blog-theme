We share the latest beauty information to those who are interested in the beauty industry.

Working in the beauty industry since 2016, we have eyewitnessed the astonishing growth in the business around the globe. Then we wondered, 'is there any specialized platform that shares the latest Korean beauty information?' The answer was, 'No.'

Right then, without any hesitation, we decided to publish a simple blog where we can share any information freely. So here we are, 'the Yuri's Pouch' for your convenience. We'll help you to get access to all the K-beauty information you have been looking for.
